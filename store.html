Mariatou's groundnut brittle About Our snaks

Even in the middle of the Gambian bush, it is possible to strive for perfection. Choosing the groundnut one by one, roasting them by hand on a firewood to give them crunchines and a bit of smoke, and finally covering them in a secret mixture of caramel and honey lightly perfumed with lemon, Mariatou has discovered magic.

But you need to be warned, it's a life choice : if you taste them you will be addicted for life.
